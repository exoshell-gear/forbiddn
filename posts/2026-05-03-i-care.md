---
title: I CARE
date: "2026-05-03"
---

I care. I care that we lost a fan. I care. It seems like I don't but I do. I care that J is having a kid. I care that V is learning to ride. I care about my credit score. I care about the customers who placed orders about JOHN. Melissa. I care about K. I DO have energy for this. I'm NOT in a rush. I cannot succeed if I don't care. I just have to try. I don't have to be perfect, I just have to try. I care about people who call me. I care what they have to say. I care about everything and everyone. And when I can't give someone my energy, I will let them know. It is time for a set of beliefs to die. I will keep some valuable lessons, but the rest don't serve me anymore. Perhaps they never did. And perhaps I misunderstood the philosophy in the first place. How do you expect to have a successful business without caring about your customers? Yes the cards are stacked against you, but you do have things within your control. You can tell that guy you'll pay him, and stop the spam, the damage. Don't be an ostrich. Your empire's reputation is your reputation.
