I had a rough week
She said I should treat myself 
Said I deserve it
But I was reluctant 
Later that night I thought I'd go to the bar
Dressed up nice and got in my car
I wanted a nice juicy buger and some fries. 
And if course some drinks to take the edge off 
I drove to the other side of the city, to try out a new joint 
Got inside sat down and opened up the menu 
And that's when she texts me 
"I don't have my keys"
I drove all the way back
To the other side of town
From the night I had planned
So she could get inside 
So she didn't have to wait outside 
On a wet chilly night
I put mine aside 
So she could get inside 
But she'd rather have flowers
